Everything a shared list should be

Built to feel instant, work anywhere, and keep your data yours.

⚡

Shared in real time

Everyone in the house sees changes the instant they happen, pushed live from your Nextcloud.

🧭

Organised by aisle

Items group into colour-coded shop areas, so you shop in order instead of doubling back.

✨

Smart input

Type “2 cups flour” — it splits the quantity, picks the right aisle, and merges duplicates for you.

📴

Works offline

Add and tick items with no signal in the shop; everything syncs the moment you're back online.

🧠

Learns your habits

Put an item in an aisle once and it remembers, so next time it lands in the right place.

↕️

Drag to reorder

Arrange items to match your store's layout — within an aisle or across areas.

🔗

Share with anyone

Share lists with Nextcloud users and groups, or a public link — password-protected if you like.

🎨

Themed to your server

The app automatically adopts your Nextcloud's own brand colour for a seamless look.

Private by design

Your shopping lists live on your Nextcloud — not ours, not anyone's. There are no accounts to create, no analytics, and no third-party servers in the middle.

  • Self-hosted: data never leaves your server
  • Sign in with secure app passwords (Login Flow v2)
  • No ads, no tracking, no telemetry
  • Open source — read every line
